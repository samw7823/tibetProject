Free Tibet

This short film is directed and edited by Sunny Zorawar and the background score is written by Robert J. P. Oberg. In just over five minutes, Sunny brings to light some of the most significant issues being faced in Tibet. In the first interview, a man explains the struggle of choosing to send one's child away to a foreign country in hopes of helping their children find a better education and freedom. With 31 likes as of December 15, this video has put faces and personal stories to the issues of Tibet.

Support for Tibet

There are many organizations around the world that work to advocate for a free Tibet. Among these organizations is Friends of Tibet, an organization with the mission of gaining independence for Tibet. Friends of Tibet features a section on media that brings to light issues in Tibet through cartoons, news artciles, among other media outlets.

Another organization that supports Tibet is the Central Tibetan Administration, Restoring Freedom for Tibetans. The website for the organization is tibet.net, where they have an entire section devoted to the Media Center, where they have archives of online resources and media regarding Tibet.

One of the most powerful tools in giving Tibetans a voice on the global stage is social media. freetibet.org calls upon its readership to take action by sharing the information. "You play a vital role in ensuring that Tibetans' struggles are heard and that a growing number of people across the world are taking action for Tibet," (Get Involved, Videos and Graphics). Free Tibet explains that "Communications in Tibet are heavily restricted and monitored, with Tibetans risking arrest and torture to send information to the outside world."

Students for a Free Tibet is an international organization that calls upon students to create changes. The vision of Students for a Free Tibet is "In our work for Tibetan independence we also aim to inspire and enable people, especially youth, to create a just and equitable world, free of oppression, in which there is respect for the earth and all living things."

International Campaign for Tibet that has resources for people to get involved in supporting Tibet's fight for independence, along withe informational resources.

The Free Tibet Campaign USA advocates for improvement through communication and education of the issues in Tibet. The Free Tibet Campaign proudly shows that it has had an online web presence for over 10 years, they "have been online supporting the Free Tibet movement through education and online campaigns." Social media and online campaigns allow Tibetans in exile, along with other people around the world, to contribute to the fight for independence in Tibet.

Another organization is Free Tibetan Heroes "is a global campaign devised to highlight the cases of Tibetan political prisoners and take action for their release." The organization asks people to send messages to political leaders. They also have a presence on twitter to send timely updates. This allows people to stay connected with the cause by receiving updated information.

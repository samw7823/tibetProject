Phayul

Phayul is a resturaunt located at ADDRESS. It famously serves Momo. When I interviewed the head chef at Phayul, he spoke to be about his experience working in New York City. One common theme at the resturaunts I visited is that a majority of the customers hear about the resturaunts through word of mouth and most many of the customers are Tibetans.

Friend's Corner Cafe

This small restaurant had delicious food, and was one of the stops along the Momo Crawl, where over 30 resturaunts were visited to determine which resturaunt had the best Momos.

GangJong Kitchen

This resturant had delicious food. After speaking to the chef and owner, I learned that since opening the resturaunt in New York, they had added certain items to the menu to have something on the menu that might appeal to non-Tibetans as well. This resturaunt was also a part of the Momo Crawl.

Amdo Kitchen New York

This is a food truck located across the street from the other resturaunt Phayul. Amdo Kitchen advertises on Foursquare and Yelp

Third Annual Momo Crawl Winner

The Momo Crawl took place on November 22, 2014 in Jackson Heights, New York, NY. The event is described in the following way on the Facebook page, "All restaurants and street vendors who sell momos in the half-mile around the Jackson Heights subway station will be offering momos for $1 each to those in possession of a 2014 momo map." 786 people replied that they attended the event on Facebook. Social media was used to help reach a wide audience for the Momo Crawl.
